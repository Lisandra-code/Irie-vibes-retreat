The Beginning

This is my husband and me with two of our three daughters. I still remember when they were so little, and now they’ve grown into amazing young women. Looking back, it’s incredible to see how far we’ve come—not just as a family but also in all that we’ve accomplished together over the years. These moments remind us of the journey we’ve shared and the love that continues to guide us.
This is a photo of me with my three daughters when they were young. They look so adorable and small here—it’s hard to believe how much they’ve grown since then! My daughters have been an integral part of our journey, contributing in their own ways to making our dream building a reality.
This is Blue Rose in its early days—here’s my husband hard at work laying the floorboards. It was one of the first rooms we finished, and it holds a special place in our hearts. We named it Blue Rose because of the many blue elements and decorations that bring the room to life. My husband handled the building and construction, while I focused on the decorating, making this space a true blend of our efforts and styles.
This is Peach Rose before we started working on it. Back then, it served as our storage room, which is why it was packed with so many things. Unlike Blue Rose and Sunshine Suite, which are duplexes, Peach Rose is a cozy studio. I’m especially proud of how this room turned out—it’s undergone such a stunning transformation! Take a look at the before and after section to see the difference. It’s no surprise this room is the most popular with our guests!
This is Sunshine Suite, one of our duplexes, and the first room we completed together. Here’s my husband hard at work putting up the wallpaper—a detail that really brings the space to life. Every time we visit France, our family stays in this duplex, Sunshine Suite, or Peach Rose. It’s always a joy to come back and see how these spaces have become such a warm and welcoming part of our journey.
My husband and I purchased this neglected and broken-down house as a dream project, drawn by its potential and the challenge it presented. Over the years, we traveled back and forth from the Netherlands to France, dedicating each trip to repairing and restoring it. Together, we poured our hearts and hands into every detail, transforming what was once a crumbling structure into a home filled with life, warmth, and memories.
